About Wings of Fire

Wings of Fire is an autobiography of Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, the former President of India. The book narrates his early life, struggles, and the experiences that shaped him into the man he became. It is an inspiring story that touches on themes of resilience, determination, and the pursuit of dreams. The autobiography also delves into his career as a scientist and his contributions to India's space and missile programs.

Genre: Autobiography, Inspirational

Published: 1999

Pages: 272 pages
